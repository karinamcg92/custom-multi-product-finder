Step 2. Pick your undertone*

 

What's your undertone?

Undertone colours explained
  • Look at your inner forearm
  • Shine a light onto your arm and try to pick out which colour is more prominent; pink, yellow, orange/red or olive. This is your undertone colour.
  • If no colour stands out then you could have a neutral skin tone.
Handy Hint
If the veins on your wrist are;
Blue or purple - then you may have a pink undertone
Blue/green - then you might have a yellow or orange/red undertone
Green or olive - then you could have an olive undertone
If you think you have both it is possible you could be neutral
Check your Jewellery
Do you suit gold or silver? Generally pink undertones look better in silver and platinum jewellery and yellow/olive tones suit gold jewellery. If you suit both you could be neutral or orange/red.

Formulation

Housed in a luxe glass bottle with a jumbo doe-foot applicator for greater control and less waste, this foundation offers highly pigmented coverage that can be layered or diffused to suit your look.
