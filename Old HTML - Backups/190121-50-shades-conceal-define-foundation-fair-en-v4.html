Step 2. Pick your undertone

What's your undertone?

This is the colour underneath the surface

WARM: If the base tone of your skin is yellow or gold, you have warm undertones.

COOL: If you see hints of blue, pink or red, then you have cool undertones.

NEUTRAL: If there's a mixture of both warm and cool hues, or your undertone is the same colour as your actual skin colour, then you fall into the neutral category.

Handy Hint

If the veins on your wrist are;

Blue or purple - you're cool toned

Blue-green - you're neutral toned

Green or olive - you're warm toned

Check your Jewellery

Do you suit gold or silver? Cool undertones look better in silver and platinum, and warm-toned look better in gold. If you suit both, you could be neutral.
